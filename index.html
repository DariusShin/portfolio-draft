About Me

Hi there! I'm Darius Lee Shin, a passionate web development learner based in Malaysia. My goal is to become a full stack developer, capable of building dynamic and responsive web applications from the ground up.

My journey in web development began with a fascination for technology and a desire to bring ideas to life through code. Currently, I am focusing on mastering the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while also exploring popular frameworks such as React and Node.js.
